Caveats

This tool intentionally ignores many real-world factors. Use it as a directional comparison, not a financial recommendation.
  • No taxes (income, Eigenmietwert, capital gains, dividend, withholding).
  • No pillar 3a contributions or indirect amortisation.
  • No inflation; all values are in nominal CHF.
  • No property appreciation or depreciation; property value held constant.
  • No maintenance, insurance, utilities, or vacancy costs.
  • No refinancing or transaction fees at renewal.
  • Single tranche only (no 1st/2nd mortgage split, no LTV-tier rate differences).
  • Amortisation is direct; balance is reduced each month.
  • Renewal: at the end of the fixed-rate term the loan rolls over at the user-specified renewal rate for the remainder of the horizon.
  • Monte Carlo uses geometric Brownian motion with a deterministic seed derived from the inputs, so results are reproducible across re-renders.
